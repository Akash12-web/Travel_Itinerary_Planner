About Visakhapatnam

Visakhapatnam, also known as Vizag, is the largest city in Andhra Pradesh. It's known for its beautiful beaches, lush green hills, and rich cultural heritage.

  • Famous beaches: RK Beach, Rushikonda
  • Scenic hills: Kailasagiri, Dolphin's Nose
  • Historical sites: Submarine Museum
  • Local cuisine: Seafood, Andhra meals
